## Patterns observed

- **Core updates** occur roughly every 2-4 months and are the most
  frequent update type.
- **Spam updates** and **link spam updates** occur periodically,
  targeting spam/manipulation tactics specifically, separate from core
  ranking changes.
- **Helpful content updates** appear as a distinct named update type
  from August 2022 through September 2023 (3 instances), then never
  again in this list — consistent with Google folding the "helpful
  content system" into core ranking systems rather than shipping it as
  a separate named update going forward.
- **Product reviews updates** (later just "reviews updates") appear from
  December 2021 through November 2023 (7 instances), then never again —
  similarly consistent with this system being absorbed into core updates.
- **Page experience update for desktop** (Feb 2022) appears once, with
  no other page-experience-specific updates in this list.
- **February 2026 Discover update** is the only Discover-specific update
  in the list.
- Two **"Ranking ongoing issue"** entries (Oct 2023, Aug 2024) are
  incident/bug reports rather than intentional ranking updates.
