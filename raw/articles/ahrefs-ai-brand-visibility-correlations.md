# Top Brand Visibility Factors in ChatGPT, AI Mode, and AI Overviews (75k Brands Studied)

Source: https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-brand-visibility-correlations/
Author: Louise Linehan (with Xibeijia Guan); reviewed by Ryan Law
Published: 2025-12-12
Fetched: 2026-07-07

## Methodology

Researchers analyzed 75,000 brands, filtering for domains with Domain
Rating (DR) > 40 and branded keywords with monthly search volume ≥ 800.
They examined millions of AI responses using Ahrefs Brand Radar,
applying Spearman correlation coefficients to identify patterns between
brand/SEO metrics and AI visibility (brand mentions) across ChatGPT, AI
Mode, and AI Overviews.

## Correlation rankings (approximate, ranges across the three platforms)

- YouTube mentions: ~0.737 (strongest correlation of any factor)
- Branded web mentions (across blog posts, anchors, video transcripts,
  descriptions, titles): 0.656–0.709
- Branded anchors (the visible, clickable link text featuring the brand
  name): 0.511–0.628
- Branded search volume (total search volume of all keywords containing
  the brand name): 0.352–0.466
- Domain Rating: 0.266–0.326
- Number of backlinks: ~0.194–0.275

"YouTube mentions show the strongest correlation with AI visibility
(~0.737), outperforming every other factor."

Content volume showed "almost no relationship" with AI visibility,
challenging programmatic content strategies. Link volume similarly
showed weak correlations.

## Platform-specific insights

- **AI Mode** shows the strongest correlations with traditional brand
  authority signals. "This pattern suggests that AI Mode acts as a sort
  of consensus engine, recommending brands that most people already
  know and search for."
- **ChatGPT** shows weaker correlations with classic SEO metrics but
  stronger correlations with advertising metrics: "Of all three AI
  systems studied, ChatGPT brand mentions correlate closest with ad
  metrics" — 0.286 for ad traffic, 0.273 for ad cost. The article notes
  this "doesn't mean ChatGPT directly rewards ad spend, but that brands
  that advertise heavily tend to dominate the kind of content that
  ChatGPT draws on."
- **AI Overviews** place slightly more emphasis on Domain Rating than
  the other two platforms.

## Brand overlap across platforms

Despite different selection tendencies per platform, significant overlap
exists in which brands get mentioned across all three: AI Overviews &
AI Mode (0.821 correlation), AI Mode & ChatGPT (0.769), AI Overviews &
ChatGPT (0.749).

## Caveats and recommendations

"The usual disclaimer applies: correlation isn't causation. We've
spotted patterns between search metrics and AI mentions, but that
doesn't mean improving these metrics will automatically boost your AI
visibility."

For smaller/challenger brands, the article recommends: building YouTube
presence, earning genuine mentions (rather than manufacturing volume),
and targeting ChatGPT specifically, since it shows the weakest
correlation with established brand authority metrics.
