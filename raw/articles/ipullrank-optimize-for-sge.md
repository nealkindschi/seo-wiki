# How to Optimize for AI Overviews (SGE)

Source: https://ipullrank.com/optimize-content-for-sge
Author: Garrett Sussman (iPullRank)
Published: 2024-02-06

## Main Claims
Optimizing for Google's AI Overviews (formerly SGE) is achievable, unlike static LLMs: "Appearing in a static Large Language Model (LLM), like ChatGPT-3.5, might be valuable for brand visibility, but it won't send traffic to your website." AI Overviews uses Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG), combining LLM output with retrieved web content, and sources unpredictably.

## Study Findings
- Authoritas study (1,000 queries): 93.8% of citations weren't in top-10 organic results.
- Mike King's study (90K queries): links sourced disproportionately from organic positions 1, 2, and 9 — in tension with the Authoritas finding.
- "The more saturated the topic or vague the query, the more volatility we'll see in the results day to day."

## Recommendations

### 1. Document & Track Performance
Spreadsheet tracking: target keywords, URLs, platforms, content relevance scores, citations, ranking positions. (Free AI Overviews Performance Tracker template offered.)

### 2. Build Keyword Strategy
- Start with high-volume entry queries
- Supplement traditional keyword research with People Also Ask queries and platform follow-up searches
- Focus on long-tail, conversational variations

### 3. Analyze Competing Content
- Identify "Fraggles" — actual text chunks used by RAG systems (not whole pages)
- Use tools like MarketBrew's AI Overviews Visualizer to assess content relevance scores
- Target similarity scores matching top-performing chunks (typically 80%+)

### 4. Content Optimization Tactics
- Improve written chunks for clarity/usefulness
- Add FAQ sections addressing specific queries
- Include information gain (unique insights unavailable elsewhere)
- Incorporate SME quotes with proper attribution
- Use fresh, relevant statistics
- Implement structured data (JSON-LD)
- eCommerce: optimize Google Shopping Graph integration

## Frameworks

### Three content format types in AI Overviews
1. Informational: paragraphs, lists, images, citations
2. Local: above + maps and Google Business listings
3. Shopping: above + product cards and listings

## Case Study Experiments
1. Enterprise SEO: 86% similarity score — no placement (as of 2024-02-05)
2. Robots.txt: 82% score bulleted list — appeared for follow-up "What are different types of robots.txt directives?"
3. Media Organization Examples: 85% score generalized examples list — appeared in AI Overviews snapshot

## Tools
Orbitwise, MarketBrew's AI Overviews Visualizer (free), SurferSEO, MarketMuse, AlsoAsked (PAA extraction), Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, Semrush.

## Caveat
Results aren't sustainable across all users — AI Overviews vary by searcher, location, time. Success = increasing likelihood of sourcing across targeted topics, not guaranteed placement.
