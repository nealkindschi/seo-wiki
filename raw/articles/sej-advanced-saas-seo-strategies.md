# 3 Advanced SaaS SEO Strategies & Best Practices

Source: https://www.searchenginejournal.com/advanced-saas-seo-strategies-best-practices/382145/
Author: Kevin Indig
Published: 2020-10-01

## Key Claims
SaaS companies face a crowded market (number of SaaS companies grew 5,000% from 2011-2020), requiring advanced, scalable strategies beyond standard tactics.

## Three Core Recommendations

### 1. Learn Hubs (Knowledge Centers)
Structured, topic-organized content libraries systematically targeting keywords within related subjects, integrating product experiences within educational content.
- Zapier's learn center: 5,000 top-10 rankings, ~50,000 monthly sessions
- Canva's resource hub: 250,000+ sessions from 5,500 top-10 keywords

### 2. Marketplace Formats
Scalable page templates for apps, integrations, or service providers.
- Shopify's app marketplace: 140,000+ monthly sessions, 3,500+ top-10 rankings
- Zapier's integration directory: 65,000+ sessions

### 3. Tools & Calculators
Linkable assets that serve as gateways to the product.
- Gusto's tool library: 2,000 backlinks, 90,000 monthly sessions
- Splunk's calculators: thousands of backlinks (5,800 for its data maturity calculator alone)

## Key Tactics
- Prioritize user value first, then optimize for SEO
- Create content addressing "alternatives to your product" rather than direct competitor comparisons
- Integrate products directly within content experiences

## Primary Challenge Identified
SaaS startups lose momentum after initial traction — conversions/traffic stagnate, teams shift to paid channels, which masks underlying product-market fit issues.
