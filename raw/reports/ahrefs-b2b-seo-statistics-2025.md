# 43 B2B SEO Statistics for 2025

Source: https://ahrefs.com/blog/b2b-seo-statistics/
Author: Si Quan Ong (reviewed by Ryan Law)
Published: 2025-11-12
Fetched: 2026-07-08

A curated compilation of third-party B2B SEO/marketing statistics,
each individually sourced by Ahrefs from the original study/report
named below. Numbers below are facts, not the compiling article's
original research (except where the source is listed as "Ahrefs").

## Traffic & organic search's role

- Organic search conversion rate: 5.0% average (Ruler Analytics).
- 27% of marketers name organic search their #1 channel (Binary
  Demand).
- B2B SaaS SEO ROI: ~702% average, ~7-month break-even over a 3-year
  horizon (First Page Sage).
- Cost per lead: $147 organic vs. $280 paid search, B2B SaaS (First
  Page Sage).
- Google delivers 345x more traffic than ChatGPT, Gemini, and
  Perplexity combined (Ahrefs).
- 96.98% of clicks occur within the top 10 organic results (Ahrefs).
- 45.7% of Google searches are branded (Ahrefs).
- AI Overviews reduce clicks to websites by 34.5% (Ahrefs).
- 26% of brands have zero mentions in AI Overviews (Ahrefs).
- 28% of ChatGPT's most-cited pages have zero Google organic
  visibility (Ahrefs).
- AI platforms show a 25.7% stronger preference for fresh content than
  traditional search (Ahrefs).
- 28.9% of AI Overview citations come from the top 50 brands in a
  category (Ahrefs).
- 76% of AI Overview citations are pulled from Google's own top-10
  organic results (Ahrefs).
- AI-referred traffic to websites grew 85% since January (Ahrefs).
- 80%+ of AI-referred traffic comes from ChatGPT specifically (Ahrefs).

## Technical SEO for B2B

- 55.6% of SEO professionals say technical SEO is undervalued
  (TripleDart).
- 40% of B2B companies lack internal technical SEO expertise
  (Statista).
- 78% of websites use schema/structured markup (W3Techs).
- 80% of B2B buyers use mobile at work; 60%+ say mobile impacted a
  recent purchase decision (BCG).
- ~40% of websites pass all Core Web Vitals thresholds (Addy Osmani).

## Content marketing & link building

- 46% of B2B marketers expect a content marketing budget increase in
  2025 (CMI).
- Biggest content challenges: conversion (40%), resource constraints
  (39%), measuring effectiveness (33%) (CMI).
- 87% of marketers use AI for content creation; AI content costs ~4.7x
  less than human-written content; average monthly spend on AI content
  tools is $188; 51% plan to increase AI-content spending (Ahrefs).
- Marketers using AI publish 42% more content on average (median 17
  vs. 12 articles/month) (Ahrefs).
- AI-generated content breakdown: 87% blog posts, 76% brainstorming,
  73% outlining, 67% content updates (Ahrefs).
- 48.6% of SEOs rate digital PR as the most effective 2025 tactic
  (Editorial.Link).
- High-quality guest posts cost $692-$957 on average (Buzzstream).
- 95% of B2B decision-makers say strong thought leadership increases
  their receptiveness to a vendor's sales approach; 51% say quality
  thought leadership helps convince executives on vendor choice; 79%
  are more likely to advocate for an RFP proposal from a consistent
  thought-leadership source (Edelman-LinkedIn B2B Thought Leadership
  Impact study).

## B2B buyer journey

- 61% of B2B buyers prefer a rep-free buying process; 73% actively
  avoid irrelevant sales outreach (Gartner).
- Shortlist size: 49% had 1-3 products shortlisted in 2024 vs. 45%
  with 4-7 products in 2023 (G2).
- 34% say research is the longest stage of buying; AI power-users
  spend relatively less time there (G2).
- 31% consult review sites more than any other information source
  (G2).
- Average B2B buying journey now spans 10 interaction channels, up
  from 5 in 2016 (McKinsey).
- Only ~5% of B2B customers are "in-market" (actively buying) at any
  given time (LinkedIn).
- 81% of buyers already have a preferred vendor before the vendor's
  first outreach (Demand Gen Report).
- Average buying group size: 10-11 stakeholders (6sense).
- Typical B2B buying cycle length: 11.5 months (6sense).
- 71% of buyers ultimately chose their very first shortlisted option
  (TrustRadius).
