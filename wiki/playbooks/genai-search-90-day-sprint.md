# 90-Day GEO Sprint

**Why/when to use this:** A project-management template for teams
starting GEO/AEO work from scratch — a 12-week, 4-phase execution plan
with weekly deliverables. Use this to sequence the tactics in
[[geo-content-optimization-tactics]] and
[[optimizing-for-the-agentic-web]] rather than attempting all of them at
once. Per [[similarweb-how-to-win-the-race-for-gen-ai-search-2026]].

## Phase 1: Foundations (Weeks 1-3)

**Goal:** define your domain, benchmark visibility, identify trusted
sources.

- **Week 1 — Map your domain**: identify main topics/subtopics; audit
  folder structure and topic hierarchy; analyze top GenAI performers in
  your niche (benchmark structure + formats); document gaps in semantic
  coverage.
  **Deliverable:** topic map + structural improvement list.
- **Week 2 — Benchmark AI visibility**: collect AI visibility,
  sentiment, and prompt samples (or existing metrics); identify strong
  vs. weak topic clusters; identify which competitors dominate each
  topic; create a prioritized list of topics needing deeper coverage.
  **Deliverable:** topic visibility dashboard.
- **Week 3 — Identify AI-trusted domains**: collect the domains/URLs
  Gen AI cites for your topics; identify repeated sources (authority
  partners); highlight missing brand-mention opportunities; prioritize
  domains by influence + prompt frequency (see
  [[geo-content-optimization-tactics]]'s third-party authority section).
  **Deliverable:** authority & citation opportunity list.

## Phase 2: Build authority (Weeks 4-6)

**Goal:** earn the citations and external signals Gen AI relies on.

- **Week 4 — Off-page authority push**: pitch expert quotes/commentary
  to key publications; contribute data/insights/reports to authority
  sites; strengthen directory listings (Crunchbase, G2, etc.); engage
  top UGC communities (Reddit, StackOverflow) with high-quality answers.
  **Deliverable:** at least 3-5 new brand mentions/placements.
- **Week 5 — Fix negative or weak sentiment**: review negative prompts +
  negative answer samples; identify outdated/misleading information;
  update own content to clarify issues; contact publishers with
  inaccuracies and request corrections; create content that reframes the
  brand narrative positively. See
  [[geo-content-optimization-tactics]]'s "Sentiment monitoring and
  correction" section for the full method.
  **Deliverable:** sentiment improvement plan.
- **Week 6 — Strengthen entity clarity**: standardize naming for
  products/features/categories; add missing entity explanations ("What
  is X? How does X work?"); create canonical pages for primary entities;
  interlink related entities to strengthen semantic depth.
  **Deliverable:** entity index + updated internal linking structure.

## Phase 3: Build AI-ready content (Weeks 7-9)

**Goal:** create content that's easy for Gen AI to reuse.

- **Week 7 — Analyze and cluster prompts**: collect real user prompts
  for your topic; break each into the five intent components (task,
  format, entity, depth, constraint — see
  [[geo-content-optimization-tactics]]); cluster prompts by pattern
  ("What is...", "How to...", "Best..."); identify gaps not yet
  addressed.
- **Week 8 — Create content matching user intent and AI structure**:
  write or update pages to directly satisfy the full intent; add a
  TL;DR (40-80 words); add a Short Answer block with bullets/steps; use
  clear H2/H3 headings mirroring user phrasing; use atomic paragraphs
  (one idea per block); include explicit entities; use predictable
  templates (definitions, comparisons, steps, FAQs); add schema markup
  where relevant.
- **Week 9 — Refine and validate for AI use**: rewrite unclear or
  ambiguous content blocks; remove fluff and improve precision; ensure
  each section is easily extractable; validate entity consistency and
  clarity.

## Phase 4: Measure, improve, scale (Weeks 10-12)

**Goal:** validate gains and expand what works.

- **Week 10 — Evaluate impact**: measure changes in visibility,
  citations, and sentiment; review which topics improved most; identify
  which tactics had the highest ROI; analyze new prompts where the brand
  now appears.
  **Deliverable:** 30-day GEO impact report.
- **Week 11 — Address persisting weaknesses**: identify remaining topic
  gaps; target additional authority partners; strengthen entity clarity
  for unclear areas; create supplemental intent-aligned content.
  **Deliverable:** updated improvement roadmap.
- **Week 12 — Systemize the process**: document a repeatable GEO
  workflow for the team; create templates/checklists for future content;
  formalize a quarterly GEO review and optimization cycle; expand to the
  next set of priority topics.
  **Deliverable:** GEO playbook v1 for ongoing operations.
