# AI Features and Your Website — Google Search Central

**Citation:** Google Search Central. "AI features and your website."
Last updated 2025-12-10 UTC.
https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/ai-features

## Key takeaways

- Reinforces [[google-ai-optimization-guide]]: "there are no additional
  requirements to appear in AI Overviews or AI Mode, nor other special
  optimizations necessary" — standard indexing/snippet eligibility is
  sufficient.
- Explains the two AI surfaces distinctly: **AI Overviews** summarize
  complex topics with links for deeper exploration; **AI Mode** handles
  queries needing "further exploration, reasoning, or complex
  comparisons," letting users ask nuanced questions that used to require
  multiple searches. Both may use "query fan-out" — issuing multiple
  related sub-queries — and can surface "a wider and more diverse set of
  helpful links" than traditional search.
- **New (not in the prior source)**: AI-feature traffic is measurable —
  it appears in Search Console's Performance report under the "Web"
  search type, and Google states clicks from AI-Overview result pages
  show higher-quality engagement.
- **New**: site owners have explicit controls to *limit or exclude*
  content from these features: `robots.txt` directives, `nosnippet`,
  `data-nosnippet`, `max-snippet`, or `noindex`.
