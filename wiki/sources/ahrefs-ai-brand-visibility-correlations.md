# Top Brand Visibility Factors in ChatGPT, AI Mode, and AI Overviews (Ahrefs)

**Citation:** Louise Linehan (with Xibeijia Guan), reviewed by Ryan Law.
"Top Brand Visibility Factors in ChatGPT, AI Mode, and AI Overviews (75k
Brands Studied)." Ahrefs Blog, 2025-12-12.
https://ahrefs.com/blog/ai-brand-visibility-correlations/

## Key takeaways

- Studied 75,000 brands (DR > 40, branded keyword volume ≥ 800/mo) using
  Ahrefs Brand Radar, applying Spearman correlations between brand/SEO
  metrics and AI-mention visibility across ChatGPT, AI Mode, and AI
  Overviews.
- **YouTube mentions correlate strongest with AI visibility (~0.737)** —
  stronger than branded web mentions (0.656–0.709), branded anchors
  (0.511–0.628), branded search volume (0.352–0.466), Domain Rating
  (0.266–0.326), or backlink count (0.194–0.275).
- **Content volume has almost no correlation** with AI visibility,
  pushing back on programmatic/high-volume content strategies.
- **Platform differences**: AI Mode correlates most with traditional
  brand-authority signals ("acts as a sort of consensus engine");
  ChatGPT correlates weakest with classic SEO authority metrics but
  *most* with ad metrics (0.286 ad traffic, 0.273 ad cost) — though the
  article cautions this likely reflects heavy advertisers dominating the
  content ChatGPT draws on, not a direct reward for ad spend; AI
  Overviews leans slightly more on Domain Rating than the others.
- **Cross-platform brand-mention overlap is high**: 0.821 (AI Overviews
  vs. AI Mode), 0.769 (AI Mode vs. ChatGPT), 0.749 (AI Overviews vs.
  ChatGPT) — i.e., largely the same brands surface across all three,
  even though (per [[muckrack-generative-pulse-ai-reading-may-2026]])
  the specific source domains cited differ substantially by provider.
- Explicit **correlation ≠ causation** caveat.
- Recommends smaller/challenger brands: build YouTube presence, earn
  genuine mentions rather than manufacturing content volume, and target
  ChatGPT specifically since it shows the weakest correlation with
  pre-existing brand authority.
