Carlos Silva et al., "SaaS SEO: An Actionable Strategy for Growth" (Semrush blog, 2024-08-06).

## Key takeaways

- The wiki's only prior SaaS-specific note was a single line in
  [[enterprise-seo-strategy]] ("attract searchers seeking the solution
  category your product provides"). This source is a full dedicated
  SaaS SEO playbook — new territory, not an extension of one line.
- **SaaS-specific reframing**: prioritize personas/problems over
  generic keywords, and conversions over traffic — a sharper framing
  than generic keyword-intent work, since a SaaS buyer journey usually
  requires demos/trials/case-study proof, not just a purchase click.
- **3-stage SaaS funnel (ToFu/MoFu/BoFu)** with concrete keyword and
  content-type examples per stage — a SaaS-flavored variant of
  [[terakeet-enterprise-seo]]'s 4-tier funnel framework (Research/
  Category/Product/Branded); the two frameworks are compatible (ToFu ≈
  Research+Category, MoFu ≈ Product, BoFu ≈ Branded/commercial-intent)
  rather than conflicting.
- **9-step build process** close in spirit to
  [[keyword-mapping-and-cannibalization]]'s process but SaaS-specific
  in content: pain-point/informational keyword research for ToFu
  authority content, commercial-intent landing-page keywords for
  product pages, and a distinct **comparison-keyword** step
  ("[Product] vs. [Competitor]" pages) — a SaaS/software-category-
  specific tactic not previously named in the wiki (though
  [[ahrefs-enterprise-seo]] mentions "vs" pages generically, this
  source ties them specifically to SaaS competitive keyword research).
- **Conversion-mechanism tactics inside content**: embedded lead-capture
  forms, internal links connecting funnel stages, and prominent
  trial/demo CTAs placed directly in educational (ToFu) content — a
  concrete example (a Pipedrive article) shows all three working
  together to move a reader from awareness to trial signup within one
  page.
- Overlaps but doesn't conflict with existing general playbooks:
  step 8 (technical SEO) is the same territory as
  [[technical-seo-audit-checklist]]; step 9 (backlinks) overlaps
  [[enterprise-seo-strategy]]'s link-building section; step 3
  (competitive intelligence) overlaps [[keyword-mapping-and-cannibalization]].
  This source's value-add is the SaaS-specific funnel/keyword framing
  layered on top, not new generic tactics.
